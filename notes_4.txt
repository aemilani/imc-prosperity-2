Algo trading:
- Premium per coupon: 637.63 SS (in the video)
- Coconut Coupons give the right to buy Coconut at a certain price at some time in future.
  Certain price = 10000 and some time in future = 250 trading days, each round is 1 trading day.
- Position limits: COCONUT: 300, COCONUT_COUPON: 600

Manual trading:
- Goldfish have heard rumours about what other inhabitants offered for their scuba gear last time.
- So, for the second bid only, they'll also consider the average of all second bids by other traders.
- They'll trade when the second bid is above the average of the the seconds bids of all participants.
- If it is below the average, the probability of the deal decreases.
